Quiet

Quiet is a tiny menu-bar app that tidies your files so you don't have to. Drag anything onto it — it figures out what you dropped, renames the mess, and puts everything where it belongs. All on your Mac.

Drag in. Stop thinking.

Drop files, folders, or screenshots onto Quiet's menu-bar icon. It handles the rest — you go back to whatever you were doing.

Feels built into your Mac

Quiet lives in the menu bar and opens like a system popover. No Electron, no sluggish web wrapper — just a native app that starts up instantly and stays out of your way.

03

Your files never leave your Mac

Everything happens on your own computer. No sign-up, no cloud storage, no one else's server reading your documents. Your organizing rules are yours to customize and keep.
